PERU

Peru's rich cultural tapestry is a reflection of its indigenous roots, Spanish colonial influence, and the contributions of various immigrant groups over the centuries. The country's population comprises a mix of indigenous peoples, mestizos (mixed indigenous and European heritage), Afro-Peruvians, and people of Asian descent, particularly those of Chinese and Japanese heritage who arrived in the 19th and 20th centuries. The historical heritage of Peru is awe-inspiring, with remnants of ancient civilizations dotting the landscape. .
